The 8 Best Free Wallpaper Sites

Explore stunning, unique wallpapers and discover hidden gems beyond Wallhaven

Stacy Fisher

The Download

  • Wallhaven is a top choice for unique and beautiful free high-resolution wallpapers.
  • DesktopNexus offers hundreds of thousands of wallpapers and resizes them to fit your screen perfectly.
  • DeviantArt provides original wallpapers, but it can be challenging to navigate due to its broad content.

If you've ever wondered where to find the best wallpapers for your computer or other device, look no further. These top picks might become your go-to sources for downloading free wallpapers.

I selected these sites based on the quality, uniqueness, and quantity of photos available. I also considered the ease of searching for wallpapers and the overall download experience.

How to Change the Wallpaper on Your Computer or Phone

We've also curated lists of the best beach wallpapers and autumn wallpapers. Perhaps you're searching for the best summer wallpaper to complement the pleasant weather.

01 of 08

Wallhaven

What We Like

  • Includes unique wallpapers not found elsewhere

  • Lots of filtering options to find specifically sized wallpapers

  • Includes a random button

  • Search for wallpapers by color

  • Extremely specific and helpful tagging system

What We Don't Like

  • Includes wallpapers not appropriate for children

Wallhaven is my favorite free wallpaper website due to its stunning and unique images that I haven't found elsewhere. There's something for everyone, from realistic to abstract designs.

You can view the wallpapers by searching by keyword, viewing the most recently added, bringing up a random page of wallpapers, selecting a color, and more. I also like that I can upload an image to find a wallpaper that's visually similar. Clicking through tags is another option; these are very detailed and accurate.

Overall, there are many ways to search for new wallpapers at Wallhaven, with numerous images to explore. I constantly find myself returning here!

Visit Wallhaven
02 of 08

DesktopNexus

What We Like

  • Automatically determines the best wallpaper size for your screen

  • 15 galleries make it easy to find specific wallpapers

  • See favorite wallpapers by other users

What We Don't Like

  • Displays ads, but you see less if you make a free user account

  • Some wallpapers are low quality

The vast collection of free wallpapers at DesktopNexus is organized so well that finding a wallpaper you like is effortless. The real benefit comes when you're ready to download, as they automatically resize to fit your screen.

Visit DesktopNexus
03 of 08

HDwallpapers.net

What We Like

  • Updates with new wallpapers every day

  • Over 20 categories of wallpapers

  • Lists the site's latest, featured, and popular wallpapers

  • Before downloading, you can choose a device, social media site, or specific resolution

What We Don't Like

  • You must wait 10 seconds to download a wallpaper

HDwallpapers.net is a free wallpaper site with numerous HD wallpapers that look great and cover a wide variety of subjects.

The ideal resolution for your device is automatically selected, or you can use the drop-down button to choose a different one.

Visit HDwallpapers.net
04 of 08

DeviantArt

What We Like

  • Includes unique categories of free wallpapers

  • Most are originals submitted by users

  • Some are made specifically for iPhones

  • There are multi-display wallpapers, too

  • Includes AI art

What We Don't Like

  • Many wallpapers are incorrectly categorized

  • Each wallpaper is available in just one size

  • Can't browse for wallpapers of a certain size

  • Not all wallpapers display the size before you download them

  • Must log in

DeviantArt offers much more than free wallpapers, which can be a mixed blessing. While you'll find unique and original wallpapers here, locating them may be challenging.

Visit DeviantArt
05 of 08

Simple Desktops

What We Like

  • High quality wallpapers

  • Stays on point with the simple designs

What We Don't Like

  • You cannot browse by specific resolution

  • Missing a search function

Simple Desktops is exactly what it seems: a collection of wallpapers with simple, clean designs that any minimalist will love. Some are super simple designs with solid backgrounds and others are a little busier but still keep it simple and stunning.

Visit Simple Desktops
06 of 08

Wallpaper Cave

What We Like

  • Fun categories

  • Downloads start immediately

  • Lets you request a wallpaper

What We Don't Like

  • Can't pick a certain size to download

  • Might run into the occasional oddity uploaded by a random user

Wallpaper Cave offers over 20 categories to help you find something, including unique ones like religion, celebrities, and superheroes. There is also a section for "other" wallpapers, providing a wide variety to choose from.

The home page shows which search terms are trending this week and showcases featured wallpapers and albums. I also like the Latest Uploaded Wallpapers page since I visit this site frequently.

On the download page, you receive an extensive wallpaper preview with a single download link. You cannot choose a specific size, but no user account is needed, and downloads start immediately.

Visit Wallpaper Cave
07 of 08

Bing Wallpaper Archive

What We Like

  • Lets you browse wallpapers by location and date

What We Don't Like

  • There isn't a search function

Every day, Bing has a new image available on their home page. This gallery puts them all together and provides download links. Archives go back to 2009.

With so many beautiful images here, choosing your favorite can be difficult.

Visit Bing Wallpaper Archive
08 of 08

Vladstudio

What We Like

  • Lots of unique wallpapers

  • Identifies your screen size for you

  • Includes dual and triple monitor wallpapers

What We Don't Like

  • Only seven premium wallpapers can be downloaded per user account

  • Very limited free options

These original wallpapers are so unique and vivid that you'll have trouble tearing your eyes away from the screen. Unfortunately, there's a limit to how many you can get for free.

This isn't your average wallpaper website, and one visit to Vladstudio will likely make you a fan. It offers desktop, mobile, and Zoom backgrounds.

Visit Vladstudio
